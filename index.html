About Us

One of my favorite activities to relieve stress is baking hence the name of this blog. It is proven scientifcly that a simple activity such as baking can make a huge difference when it comes to relieving stress and also helps to unleash creativity. This blog is dedicated to sharing my favorite baking recipes.
